Summary

Single-cell genomics researcher with 5 years of combined assay development and computational analysis on large scale datasets. Build analytical pipelines delivering both QC and biological insights facilitating technology development and translational medicine studies.

Core Skills

  • Wet lab & Platforms: scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, scMultiome library construction & quality controls; 10x Genomics Chromium; molecular biology (FACS, PCR, Western blot); Illumina NextSeq 500/550, NovaSeq, MiSeq
  • Dry lab tools: R/RStudio, Python, Bash, High Performance Computing

Experience

Graduate Student Researcher — Cusanovich Lab, University of Arizona, Dept. of Cellular & Molecular Medicine

2020–present
  • Developed a high-throughput scRNA-seq method achieving ~5× higher throughput than 10x Genomics Chromium with reduced per-cell cost; validated on 293T and NIH/3T3 cell lines.
  • Built “mTEA-seq,” a user-friendly scMultiome (RNA+ATAC) workflow, increasing throughput for ~2x and lowering per-sample cost; validated via computational simulation and human PBMCs.
  • Applied mTEA-seq at population scale to an insulin-defined asthma endotype (75 samples, 54 donors): integrated RNA/ATAC, performed genotyping from single-cell data, mapped cis-QTLs, and linked variants to chromatin accessibility and transcriptional changes.
  • Extended to early-life asthma origins (42 infants), identifying environment-associated immune signatures with integrated scRNA-seq and scATAC-seq analyses.
  • Cross-project analytics: supported within- and cross-lab studies; standardized QC, batch correction, and data integration workflows; built and implemented Slurm-based pipeline.

Graduate Student Researcher — Lu Lab, University of Southern California, Dept. of Pharmacy

2019–2020
  • Established chromatin-associated RNA fractionation/enrichment for interaction/structure assays; validated products with Bioanalyzer and qPCR.
  • Performed molecular engineering: primer design, plasmid construction, Cas9/gRNA efficiency testing, gel electroporation; derived monoclonal cell lines.
  • Assisted in constructing a double-knockout human iPSC line.

Undergraduate Researcher — Liu Lab, Sichuan University, Dept. of Chemical Engineering

2016–2017
  • Evaluated myricetin effects on tyrosinase activity using L-DOPA substrate; performed UV–vis kinetic analyses.
